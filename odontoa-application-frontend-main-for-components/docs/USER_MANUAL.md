## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Access the Application**
   - Open your web browser
   - Navigate to your Odontoa application URL
   - You'll be redirected to the login page

2. **Initial Login**
   - Enter your provided email address
   - Enter your temporary password
   - Click "Sign In"
   - You'll be prompted to change your password on first login

3. **Password Change**
   - Enter your current password
   - Create a new strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm your new password
   - Click "Update Password"

### System Requirements

- **Browser**: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection required
- **Screen Resolution**: Minimum 1024x768 (1920x1080 recommended)
- **JavaScript**: Must be enabled

## Authentication

### Login Process

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. If credentials are correct, you'll be redirected to the dashboard

### Password Management

#### Changing Password
1. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. Select "Change Password"
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click "Update"

#### Forgot Password
1. On the login page, click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

### Logout
1. Click on your profile icon
2. Select "Logout"
3. You'll be redirected to the login page

## Patient Management

### Adding a New Patient

1. **Navigate to Patients**
   - Click "Patients" in the main menu
   - Click "Add Patient" button

2. **Fill Patient Information**
   - **Personal Information**:
     - Full name (required)
     - Date of birth (required)
     - Gender
     - Country
     - City
     - Email address
     - Phone number (required)
     - Address

3. **Save Patient Record**
   - Click "Save" to create the patient record
   - The patient will be added to your patient list

### Patient Search and Filtering

#### Search Options
- **Search**: Type name, phone number or anything related to patient to find specific patients

### Patient Profile

#### Basic Information
- View personal details

#### Medical History
- **Dental History**: Previous treatments and procedures
- **Medical Conditions**: Current health status

#### Treatment Records
- **Treatment Plans**: Current and completed treatments
- **Appointment History**: Past appointments and notes
- **Clinical Assessments**: Orthodontic and dental evaluations

### Tabs in Patient Details

When you open a patient's details, you will see six main tabs at the top of the page. Each tab provides access to a different aspect of the patient's record:

1. **Patient Overview (Pregled pacijenta)**
   - View the patient's basic information, age, date of birth, contact details, and a summary of their record.
   - See a quick overview of the patient's status and recent activity.

2. **Therapies and Appointments (Terapije i termini)**
   - See a list of therapies and treatments the patient has received or is scheduled to receive.

3. **X-Ray Images (X-Ray snimci)**
   - Access and review all X-ray images uploaded for the patient.
   - Upload new X-ray images and add descriptions or notes.

4. **Photographs (Fotografije)**
   - View and manage clinical photographs of the patient (intraoral, extraoral, etc.).
   - Upload new photos.
   - Useful for tracking progress, documenting cases, and before/after comparisons.

5. **Orthodontic Record (Ortodontski karton)**
   - Access the patient's orthodontic chart and records.
   - Enter and review orthodontic assessments, study model and cephalometric analyses.

6. **Dental Record (Stomatološki karton)**
   - View the patient's dental chart (odontogram) and dental history.
   - Record findings for each tooth, mark conditions, and add notes.
   - Track all general dental treatments and procedures performed.

## Appointment Scheduling

### Calendar View

#### Navigation
- **Month View**: Overview of entire month
- **Week View**: Detailed weekly schedule
- **Day View**: Hour-by-hour daily schedule

#### Calendar Controls
- **Date Navigation**: Use arrows to move between dates
- **Today Button**: Quickly return to current date
- **View Toggle**: Switch between calendar views

### Creating Appointments

1. **Select Date and Time**
   - Click on desired date in calendar
   - Click on time slot
   - Or use "New Appointment" button

2. **Fill Appointment Details**
   - **Patient**: Select from patient list or search
   - **Dentist**: Choose treating dentist
   - **Date**: Confirm appointment date
   - **Time**: Set start time
   - **Duration**: Select appointment length (30, 60, 90, 120 minutes)

   - **Vrsta zahvata (Treatment Type)**: Choose treatment category
     - **Stomatološki**: General dentistry procedures
     - **Ortodontski**: Orthodontic procedures
   - **Tretman (Treatment)**: Choose specific treatment based on category
     - **Stomatološki treatments**:
       - Examination
       - Extraction
       - Filling
       - Root Canal
       - Cleaning
       - Prosthetics
       - Other
     - **Ortodontski treatments**:
       - Orthodontic Examination
       - Braces Installation
       - Check-up
       - Wire Change
       - Retention
       - Taking Impressions
       - Other
   - **Notes**: Add any special instructions or notes

3. **Save Appointment**
   - Click "Save" to create appointment
   - Appointment will appear on calendar

### Managing Appointments

#### Edit Appointment
1. Click on appointment in calendar
2. Click "Edit" button
3. Modify appointment details
4. Click "Save Changes"

#### Appointment Status
- **Scheduled**: Confirmed appointment
- **In Progress**: Currently happening
- **Completed**: Finished appointment
- **Canceled**: Canceled appointment

### Appointment Notifications

#### Automatic Reminders
- **9 AM Day Before**: Email/SMS reminder sent at 9:00 AM the day before the appointment

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems
**Issue**: Can't log in
**Solution**:
1. Check email and password
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Contact administrator

#### Slow Performance
**Issue**: Application is slow
**Solution**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Restart browser

#### Appointment Not Saving
**Issue**: Appointment disappears after saving
**Solution**:
1. Check required fields
2. Verify date/time selection
3. Ensure patient is selected
4. Try refreshing page

### Error Messages

#### "Session Expired"
- Log out and log back in
- Check system time settings
- Contact administrator if persistent

#### "Permission Denied"
- Contact administrator for access
- Check user role permissions
- Verify login credentials

#### "Data Not Found"
- Refresh the page
- Check search criteria
- Verify data exists
